**🎵 Song 2: _“Dragged Out”_** _(Vignette Approach)_ **[Verse 1]** The days blurred into weeks, Hope turned into ache. I waited for the ending, But it wouldn’t break. **[Pre-Chorus]** I was pregnant, but not really, Still I felt the weight. A cruel trick of biology, A slow and silent fate. **[Chorus]** Dragged out, like a storm that never clears, Dragged out, through the quiet and the tears. I held on, though I didn’t understand, What it means to lose without a hand. **[Verse 2]** I didn’t know the terms, But I knew the pain. I searched for answers, But none came. **[Bridge]** Even false beginnings leave a mark, Even empty arms feel dark. **[Outro]** Dragged out, still I grieve, Dragged out, still believe.
