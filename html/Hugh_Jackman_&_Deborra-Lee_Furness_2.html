**🎵 Song 2: _“Not Easy”_** _(Vignette Approach)_ **[Verse 1]** Curtains drawn, scripts unread, We whispered names inside our heads. The stage was set, but we stepped back, No spotlight for this heart attack. **[Pre-Chorus]** It was painful, not easy, No lines to memorize. Just raw emotion, And silent cries. **[Chorus]** Not easy, but we tried, Not easy, still we cried. We held each other through the storm, Even when the world felt torn. **[Verse 2]** We grieved in private, But never broke. We spoke in glances, In every stroke. **[Bridge]** We found our rhythm in the quiet, We found our strength in the fall. **[Outro]** Not easy, still we stand, Not easy, hand in hand.
