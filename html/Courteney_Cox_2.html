**🎼 Song 2: “Kept Trying” _(Vignette Approach)_** **Epigraph**: “I just kept trying.” **[Verse 1]** The piano plays a minor chord, Like the ache I couldn’t name. I smiled through the interviews, But nothing felt the same. **[Pre-Chorus]** I kept trying, Even when the light grew dim. **[Chorus]** Kept trying, through the tears, Kept trying, through the years. Every loss carved a space, Every hope found its place. **[Verse 2]** Coco came, a light so bright, But the shadows still remain. **[Bridge]** I carry them in silence, In every refrain. **[Outro]** Kept trying, And love remained.
