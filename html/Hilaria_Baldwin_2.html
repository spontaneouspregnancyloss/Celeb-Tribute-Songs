**🎵 Song 2: _“Not Fine”_** _(Vignette Approach)_ **[Verse 1]** I smiled for the camera, But my heart was torn. They asked how I was doing, I said, “I’m fine,” like I’d been sworn. **[Pre-Chorus]** But inside I was breaking, A mother without her child. I wanted to scream the truth, But I just nodded and smiled. **[Chorus]** I’m not fine, Though I wear the mask. I’m not fine, Don’t even ask. I’m healing, yes, But slowly, painfully, I confess. **[Verse 2]** I had an attachment, To someone I never met. I planned a life around you, Now I live with regret. **[Bridge]** Later miscarriage, Still a birth. Still a name, Still a worth. **[Outro]** I’m not fine, But I’m still here. I’m not fine, But I’m sincere.
