**🎵 Song 2: _“Every Second”_** _(Vignette Approach)_ **[Verse 1]** Every second of my life was new, My body changed, my mind did too. I thought of choice, I thought of fate, I thought of love that came too late. **[Chorus]** Every second, I remembered, What it meant to lose. Every second, I surrendered, To a truth I didn’t choose. **[Verse 2]** I had a great pregnancy, But the past still spoke. Two losses carved their names, In every breath I broke. **[Bridge]** I was alone, I was afraid, But I found the strength to stay. **[Outro]** Every second, Still carries you today.
